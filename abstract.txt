ABSTRACT:
In the current digital recruitment ecosystem, students often struggle to find relevant internship opportunities due to
information overload and inefficient keyword-based matching systems. These limitations lead to poor-quality
matches, wasted time, and low recruiter response rates, while recruiters face increased effort in screening unsuitable
applications.
InternSwipe addresses this issue by introducing an intelligent, swipe-based internship recommendation and
matching platform. Students and recruiters can express interest through left and right swipe actions, and a match is
created only when both parties show mutual interest. This approach significantly reduces irrelevant applications
and improves matching efficiency.
The platform integrates Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning techniques, including career trajectory
analysis to align internships with long-term career goals, skill gap identification to highlight missing skills with
learning suggestions, and behavior-aware intelligence to analyze swipe patterns and reduce swipe fatigue. The
system is implemented as a full-stack web application with secure authentication, RESTful APIs, and scalable
architecture.
InternSwipe enhances internship discovery by providing personalized, meaningful matches, improving student
career clarity and recruiter satisfaction, making it suitable for academic and real-world use.
